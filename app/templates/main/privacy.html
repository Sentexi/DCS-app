Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Debattierclub Stuttgart e.V. (“we”, “us”, or “DCS”) processes your personal data when you use our DebateHub web app at https://app.debattierclub-stuttgart.de. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

Personal data collected

  • Account registration: first name, last name, email address, and a password (securely hashed) are collected when you create an account. These details are required to verify your identity and provide access.
  • Debate participation and scoring: your debate scores (as speaker or judge), attendance, speaker rankings, and role history are recorded.
  • Topic voting: your votes in debate topic selections are stored to personalize session planning and analyze preferences.
  • Usage metadata: timestamps of logins, role preferences, and optionally provided data like judging confidence and language skills.

Purpose and legal basis

Your data is processed to manage user accounts, assign debate roles, track performance, and improve community operations. Legal bases include:

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – contract performance (account management and event participation),
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interests (improving platform quality, ensuring fair matchups, enabling communication).

Retention

Data is stored while your account is active and required for club operations. You may request deletion at any time. Unconfirmed registrations are deleted after a reasonable period.

User rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your data. You may also request data portability. To exercise your rights, contact us at vorstand@debattierclub-stuttgart.de.

Security measures

Passwords are hashed securely. Communication with the server is encrypted. Access is restricted to authorized administrators. Data minimization is practiced to reduce risk.

Data sharing

We do not sell or disclose your data to third parties unless required by law or necessary for hosting/email delivery. Our providers are contractually bound to uphold data protection standards.

Controller

The data controller under the GDPR is:
Debattierclub Stuttgart e.V.
Marktplatz 18, 71691 Freiberg am Neckar, Germany
Email: vorstand@debattierclub-stuttgart.de

Complaints

You have the right to file a complaint with a data protection authority. In Germany, this is typically the state data protection authority (Landesdatenschutzbeauftragter).

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.
